---
title: "Graph Theory and Network Topologies"
description: "Graphs with nodes and edges to try out yourself"
---

Live: https://graphs.tinfo.space/
Repo: https://github.com/HansTydecks/network-topology

Learners can add nodes and connect them with edges. This way they discover different topologies. Data flow can be observed at the touch of a button. There is a BFS and DFS simulation. Failures can be simulated. Various analysis tools are available. The different topologies are described in detail after discovery.

Teachers are encouraged to develop material themselves to best integrate the simulation into lessons.
